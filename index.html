Overview

Sefyra ("the app", "we", "our") is a free, open-use Android application that enables direct peer-to-peer file transfers between devices on the same local Wi-Fi network. This Privacy Policy explains what information is involved when you use Sefyra, and confirms that we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

Sefyra does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials of any kind
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to third parties
  • The content or metadata of files you transfer
  • Device identifiers sent to any remote server

How Sefyra Works

All file transfers happen entirely on your local network, directly between devices. No data is routed through our servers — because we don't have any.

Sefyra uses UDP broadcast on your local Wi-Fi to discover nearby devices also running the app. Once a connection is established, files are transferred via TCP directly between the two devices. This process never leaves your local network.

Permissions Used

Sefyra requests only the permissions necessary for its core functionality:

  • Wi-Fi & Network Access — required to discover devices and transfer files on the local network
  • Nearby Devices (Android 12+) — required by the OS for local network peer discovery
  • Read/Write Storage — required to read files you choose to send and save files you receive

No permission data is shared externally. All permission usage stays on your device.

Third-Party Services

Sefyra does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting SDKs. There are no ads in the app.

Children's Privacy

Sefyra does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal data whatsoever, it presents no risk to children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy — for instance, if we add new features — we will update the effective date above. Significant changes will be communicated through app update notes on the Play Store. Continued use of Sefyra after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
